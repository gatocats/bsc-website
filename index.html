Business Student Council

Background

The Business Student Council (BSC) fosters relationships within the COB, connecting faculty and Registered Student Organizations (RSOs) while developing student leaders through professional development. Our goal is to equip members with the tools needed to excel as leaders and professionals. We represent a diverse group of students across all business majors and organizations.
