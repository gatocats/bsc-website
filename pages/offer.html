Meetings

Our meetings feature guest speakers who discuss key business topics and provide insights into the industry. Additionally, we host sessions focused on professionalism, while offering members opportunities to interact and engage in important discussions.

Events

BSC attends professional events both within and outside of UTSA, giving members valuable insights into the business world. We also organize social events where members can connect and interact in a more relaxed, non-professional setting.

Opportunities

BSC offers members the chance to travel for business events, gaining firsthand experience in the industry. Additionally, we provide exclusive access to internships, as well as numerous leadership-building opportunities to help members grow professionally.

Committees

BSC has three committees—marketing, operations, and finance—each with a distinct role in ensuring the smooth functioning of the organization. These committees work together to support BSC's overall goals and ensure effective execution of events and initiatives.
