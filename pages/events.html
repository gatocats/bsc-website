Socials

We host occasional social events outside of regular meetings, allowing members to interact in a casual setting. We provide transportation for members who may have difficulty attending, ensuring everyone can participate. These socials help foster connections and build a stronger community among members.

Professional Development

BSC offers professional development through workshops, guest speakers, and networking events. Members gain valuable skills and insights to prepare for future careers. These experiences enhance leadership, communication, and problem-solving abilities.

Volunteering

BSC strives to engage with the San Antonio community by partnering with local businesses for events and fundraisers. These opportunities allow members to give back while gaining valuable experience in teamwork and leadership. Additionally, we host fundraisers to support BSC initiatives, helping fund future trips and events.
